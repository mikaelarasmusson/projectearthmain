HAPPY NEWS!

WHAT IS HAPPY NEWS?

Amid the pressing concerns of our planet's health, there are heartening developments that remind us of our capacity for positive change. The world is witnessing a remarkable transformation in the battle against global earth problems. With the rapid expansion of renewable energy sources, we are significantly reducing our carbon footprint. Reforestation initiatives are rejuvenating vital forests and ecosystems, proving that nature's resilience can be harnessed for the greater good. Across the globe, plastic waste reduction efforts are taking hold, making our oceans cleaner and safer for marine life. In our urban centers, the air is becoming easier to breathe as cities embrace cleaner transportation and greener infrastructure. Moreover, international cooperation is fostering a sense of unity and shared responsibility, with countries working together to address the pressing issue of climate change. These positive strides inspire hope that, collectively, we can surmount the global earth problems and pave the way for a more sustainable and harmonious future.

LESS FOSSIL FUELS!

Fossil energy generated by fossil fuels in the EU hit a record low in the first half of 2023, a new report has shown. In the first half of 2023, the 27 member states burned 17 percent less fossil fuels for electricity than during the same period in 2022.

MAINE' PUFFINS ARE RECOVERING!

After a rough 2021, the species had a second successful rebound year for fledgling chicks. The sea birds’ resilience and ability to reproduce, despite increasing threats due to climate change, is a glimmer of hope for scientists.

LESS POLLUTION FROM INDUSTRIES!

Energy-efficiency opportunities can help the industry cut its climate pollution by as much as 50% by 2030, as scientists say every industry must in order to ward off climate change’s worst impacts.
