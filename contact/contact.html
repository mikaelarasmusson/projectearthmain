FREQUENTLY ASKED
QUESTIONS (FAQ)

What can I expect from Project Earth's content?

Prepare yourself for an emotionally varied experience as we embark on an in-depth examination of climate-related subjects. Our discussion will encompass the grim realities of natural disasters and climate change, punctuated with moments of hope.

What can I do to help?

We encourage everyone to check our website regularly and also follow us on our other social media like Instagram! Additionally, you could donate to our friends to make sure that your money goes to a good cause.

Who are the people behind Project Earth?

We're 16 students from Malmö University who had an idea during a group project in 2023. We recognized that fostering concern for the environment proves to be a more daunting task than one would believe.
