ABOUT US

ABOUT US

Welcome to our corner of the digital world, where we tackle the formidable challenge of climate change with a healthy dose of realism and a dash of sarcasm. We are “Project Earth" – a community of young university students who recognize the gravity of the environmental crisis and believe it's time for people to grasp the seriousness of climate change and how noticeable it already is.

OUR APPROACH TO CLIMATE CHANGE

At Project Earth, we acknowledge that the climate crisis is no laughing matter. While we might add a touch of sarcasm here and there, our mission is deadly serious – to confront the urgent issue of climate change with thoughtful action and informed discussion. We believe that knowledge is power. Our platform is dedicated to sharing well-researched information about natural disasters, as well as bringing some positive news to the table. The effects of climate change are real - and are being experienced throughout the world today. We are serious about our commitment to the climate, because its repercussions directly threaten our lives.

OUR TEAM: A PROJECT GROUP WORKING AGAINST IMPENDING DOOM

We are a team of 16 young adults studying at Malmö University and together we decided to create a website to show other adults that it’s very important to not just talk about climate change as a thing of the future, but also of the present. Our mission was founded during a group project in 2023 where we realized how fundamental it is to spark people's actual interest in making a difference by holding corporations accountable, and not just by recycling your old water bottles

JOIN US: TOGETHER, WE CAN MAKE A DIFFERENCE

If you're looking for a community that combines a realistic perspective on climate change with a commitment to taking action, you've found your place. At Project Earth, we're serious about creating a more sustainable future, but we're not above using a touch of sarcasm to keep things relatable

Remember, we may face a daunting challenge, but it's one we can tackle together. Join us in the fight against climate change and be part of the solution. Together, we can make a difference
